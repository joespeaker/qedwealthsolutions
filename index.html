Our Core Beliefs

We believe that everyone, no matter
where they live, should have access
to high quality financial plannning.

We believe the value of working with a
financial planner should exceed
the cost of doing so.

We believe that a well executed plan
leads to lower levels of stress
and higher levels of happiness.

We believe that fee-based financial
planning leads to the least possible
conflicts of interest.

We believe that financial planning
should be inherently personal, not
the boiler-plate advice you may
receive at "chain" firms.

We believe in paying every dollar
of taxes owed, just don't leave
Uncle Sam a tip.

We believe that, for the most part,
investments should be "boring".

We believe that risk management
is essential to a comprehensive
financial plan.

We believe the best financial plans
contain a healthy amount of flexibility.
