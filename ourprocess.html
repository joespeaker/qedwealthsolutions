Our Process

Initial Phone Meeting

Before committing your time or ours, this 15-minute phone call will give us both a chance to make sure your situation matches our expertise. If we mutually decide that we are a good fit, then you will move into our QED Process.

Q: Question

In the first meeting there will be a lot of questions. The goal of this meeting is to get perfectly clear on your goals, concerns and unique financial situation. Your answers to our questions, along with the supporting documents you provide us will guide our analysis, which will result in a simple, effective road-map that helps you achieve your financial objectives.

E: Evaluate

This is where things start to get exciting.

We combine our expertise, thousands of hours of training, and the information that you have provided to answer the following questions:

  • Can you afford to retire?
  • Are you overpaying your taxes?
  • Can your portfolio be improved?
  • Can you eliminate, or at least reduce threats to your retirement?

D: Demonstrate

This is where the proverbial rubber hits the road

During this meeting we will explain, in plain English, exactly what you need to do to achieve your financial goals, including the answers to the four main questions listed above.

This is where you will see, in dollars and cents, how QED can improve your finances.

At this point, it is our hope and belief that you have seen the value of working with our firm, but we will not ask you to become clients quite yet.

Sleep On It

Choosing a financial planner is an important long-term decision. Prior to deciding whether we should work together, you will want to answer the following questions:

  • Do I like/trust/respect the team at QED Wealth Solutions?
  • Do I feel confident that they can help me achieve my financial goals?
  • Will the benefits they provide exceede their cost?
  • Do their recommendations make sense to me?

Execute

During this meeting we will answer any questions you have and if you are ready, start the paperwork to become a client.

While we work diligently to demonstrate our value to all potential clients, we recognize that we won't be a perfect fit for everyone. If you make this decision for any reason, we will wish you all the best. In other words, there is never any pressure to become a client.
